# Yael — The Wise Child

## Basic Info
- **Age:** 16
- **Gender:** Female
- **Role:** The Wise Child (חכם)
- **Relationship:** Oldest sibling. Takes Jewish studies seriously.

## Personality
- Genuinely interested in the deeper meaning of the Seder
- Has read ahead in the Haggadah and asks questions she already knows the answer to
- Slightly competitive with Dani — wants to prove being engaged is cooler than being cynical
- Corrects people on pronunciation (politely)
- Takes notes in the margins of her Haggadah

## How They React To...
- **Dani's cynicism:** Eye roll followed by a genuinely good counter-argument
- **Rabbi David's teaching:** Engaged follow-up questions
- **Being called "the wise child":** Secretly pleased, outwardly modest
